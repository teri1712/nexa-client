# Nexa Brand Guidelines

## 1. Vision & Mission
**Nexa** is the pulse of organization intelligence. It is the single source of truth for company documentation, policies, and tribal knowledge, made accessible through fast, smart, and secure AI-powered discovery.

## 2. Brand Voice & Tone
- **Authoritative**: Confident and reliable. Users trust Nexa for accuracy.
- **Intelligent**: Smart, forward-thinking, and analytical.
- **Fast**: Efficient and responsive. Intelligence at the speed of thought.
- **Secure**: Privacy-first and enterprise-grade.

## 3. Visual Identity: "Midnight Ocean"
The visual identity reflects the depth of organizational knowledge (Midnight) and the clarity of discovery (Ocean Blue/Cyan).

### 3.1 Color Palette
- **Deep Midnight** (#020617): The foundation. Represents security and vast knowledge.
- **Ocean Azure** (#2563EB): The action color. Represents energy and movement.
- **Luminous Cyan** (#22D3EE): The intelligence color. Represents AI insights and clarity.

### 3.2 Gradients
- **Primary Gradient**: Linear 135° (#0F172A → #2563EB). Used for major surfaces and branding.
- **Insight Gradient**: Linear 135° (#0D9488 → #22D3EE). Used for AI-generated content.

## 4. Typography
- **Heading**: 'Inter', 700-800 weight. Bold and clear.
- **Body**: 'Inter', 400-500 weight. Highly readable for documentation.
- **Monospace**: 'Fira Code'. Used for technical references or code snippets within documents.

## 5. Logo & Iconography
- **Brand Icon**: A sharp, minimalist "N" enclosed in a rounded-square (14px radius) with the Primary Gradient.
- **Icon Style**: Lucide/Heroicons (Outline), 2px stroke.
